Level 1

Novice

Water confidence and first strokes

Complete beginners aged 5–12 who are new to the water or have limited swimming experience. No prior skill required.

What You'll Learn

  • Water confidence and safety awareness
  • Floating and breath control
  • Introduction to freestyle and backstroke
  • Basic pool etiquette and lane discipline

Outcome

Swimmers graduate with the confidence to swim 25m unassisted and the foundation to progress to Intermediate level.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should my child bring to the first session?

A swimsuit, goggles, swim cap, towel, and a water bottle. We recommend arriving 10 minutes early for orientation.

Can my child join mid-term?

Yes! We accept new swimmers at any time. Our coaches will assess your child's level and place them in the appropriate group.

What happens if we miss a session?

Missed sessions can be made up within the same week, subject to availability. Please notify your coach in advance if possible.

Are there female coaches available?

Yes, we have both male and female certified coaches. Please let us know your preference when enrolling and we'll accommodate where possible.

Is there parking at the pool?

Yes, there is ample parking available at our training venues. Specific directions are shared upon enrolment.

How do I know which programme is right for my child?

If you're unsure, book a trial session and our coaches will assess your child's ability and recommend the best programme. You can also WhatsApp us for advice.
